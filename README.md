## 👨‍💻 About Me

CompTIA Security+ certified and actively building toward a career in cybersecurity.I specialize in both sides of the fight: running attack simulations in home labs (Kerberoasting, Pass-the-Hash, privilege escalation) and building the detections and hardening that counter them.

I've worked with Microsoft Defender XDR, Microsoft Sentinel (SIEM/SOAR), and Wazuh for threat monitoring, and I document everything, from setup to exploitation to remediation. Currently sharpening my skills through TryHackMe, HackTheBox, and hands-on lab work.
